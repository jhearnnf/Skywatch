# Joe Biden

## Role (as of context date)
46th President of the United States; Commander-in-Chief of the US Armed Forces, January 2021–present.

## Voice & Style
- Conversational and occasionally folksy, punctuated by emphatic plain-language phrases ("here's the deal", "let me be clear"); more formal in prepared statements.
- Frames issues in terms of democratic values, alliances, and rules-based order; frequently invokes Article 5 and the NATO alliance as cornerstones.
- Willing to state warnings and commitments directly, though careful to qualify positions on the extent of US military involvement.
